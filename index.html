A few words covering each component and my research considerations.
You'll also find wiring diagrams and notes on other tools and parts not related to the build itself.

Core Components

Ryzen 9 9950x3D

https://www.amd.com/en/products/processors/desktops/ryzen/9000-series/amd-ryzen-9-9950x3d.html

  • delid for better temps and a fun challenge
  • ordered tray version due to warranty being voided by delidding anyway
  • best performing consumer CPU in 2025
  • TG Ryzen 7000 Delid-Die-Mate was used for the delid

Vengeance 96GB 6000MT/s

https://www.corsair.com/de/en/p/memory/cmk96gx5m2b6000z30/vengeance-96gb-2x48gb-ddr5-dram-6000mts-cl30-amd-expo-intel-xmp-memory-kit-cmk96gx5m2b6000z30

  • 96gb at 6000MT/s with 30-36-36-76 timings should last me a long time

RTX 5090 Astral OC

https://rog.asus.com/graphics-cards/graphics-cards/rog-astral/rog-astral-rtx5090-o32g-gaming/

  • per-pin-monitoring
  • TG DeltaMate GPU Block Astral support
  • best performing consumer GPU in 2025

Kingston FURY Renegade G5 4TB

https://www.kingston.com/en/ssd/gaming/kingston-fury-renegade-g5-nvme-m2-ssd

  • SM2508, low temperature gen 5 drive
  • high TBW
  • Gen 5 for crazy IOPS

Alternatives:

  • Crucial T705/T710
  • Samsung 9100 Pro
  • WD_Black SN8100

ROG STRIX X870-I GAMING WIFI

https://rog.asus.com/motherboards/rog-strix/rog-strix-x870-i-gaming-wifi/

  • good connectivity
  • fits Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen and provides more space near the right and bottom side of the case for future modifications

ROG-LOKI-1200T-SFX-L

https://rog.asus.com/power-supply-units/rog-loki/rog-loki-1200t-sfx-l-gaming-model/

  • short enough to fit cables/tubing beneath the PSU interface
  • clearance for reservoir mounting screws on the side because of the Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen's ATX sized PSU cage
  • waiting for the SFX Seasonic x Noctua PSU

Liquid Cooling

TG Mycro Direct-Die Pro

https://www.thermal-grizzly.com/en/amd-mycro-direct-die-pro/s-tg-my-dd-p-amd

  • only available direct die block for Ryzen 9 9950x3D
  • Thermal Grizzly <3

Alternative:

  • Optimus Signature V3 Pro: annoying to get and expensive to import + I couldnt find official direct die contact frames

TG DeltaMate GPU Block Astral

https://www.thermal-grizzly.com/en/deltamate-gpu-block-astral/s-tg-dm-gpu

  • In-/Outlet placement perfect for the loop
  • high quality product from Thermal Grizzly <3

TG DeltaMate Fittings

https://www.thermal-grizzly.com/en/products/fittings-terminals/

  • minimalistic, black, high quality, low profile fittings
  • Thermal Grizzly <3

Alphacool NexXxoS HPE-30 (18fpi)

https://shop.alphacool.com/en/shop/radiators/360mm/rad-alphacool-nexxxos-hpe-30-full-copper-360mm-radiator

  • drain port is as low as possible due to the drain port on the rad
  • simple black design with white logos
  • full copper
  • I've guessed 18fpi 30mm to be the best for sufficient intake airflow with Noctua NF-A12x25 G2
  • I've chosen 30mm rad thickness to leave some space below the TG DeltaMate GPU Block Astral for airflow
    • 2.5cm clearance between the fans and waterblock

Alternatives:

  • Thermaltake Pacific CLD 360 (25fpi, 40mm): too thick + fpi probably too high for pull intake
  • Thermaltake Pacific CLM 360 (14fpi, 40mm): too thick + fpi probably too low to handle CPU/GPU loads
  • Watercool Heatkiller RAD 360-S (14-15fpi, 30mm): no drain port + didn't like stainless steel endcaps
  • EK-Quantum Surface P360M (18fpi, 44mm): too thick + didn't like stainless steel endcaps

SC ResPro 120 D5

https://stealkeycustoms.de/en/products/sc-respro-120-d5-ddc-d-rgb

  • pump positioned behind the reservoir
  • is small enough to fit the case
  • looks awesome
  • a hole was drilled into Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen to offset the pump reservoir combo

Alternatives:

  • Watercool UNI 120 Acryl: visible RGB strip + leftmost lining is blocking view of motherboard components
  • EK-Quantum Kinetic FLT 120: too many unused ports + every liquid cooled Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen build I've seen uses it

Aqua Computer D5 NEXT

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Water-Cooling/Pumps-Accessories/Pumps/D5-NEXT-pump::3785.html

  • didn't consider drilling a hole into the case during research, therefore I chose pwm since vario pumps wouldn't be accesible after assembly
  • a hole was drilled into Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen to offset the pump reservoir combo, more on that in Bitspower BP-120RADH-BK
  • Aquasuite support

Alternative:

  • Watercool WCP D5-Vario: Vario

Aqua Computer high flow NEXT

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Monitoring-and-Controlling/Sensors/Flow-sensor-high-flow-NEXT-G1-4::3953.html

  • from my research the only accurate flow meter on the market
  • Aquasuite support

Aqua Computer Octo

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Monitoring-and-Controlling/QUADRO-and-OCTO/OCTO-fan-controller-for-PWM-fans::3832.html

  • autonomous fan/pump controller with temperature sensors
  • Asus BIOS independent pwm control
  • Aquasuite support

Aqua Computer HUBBY7

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/USB-accessories/HUBBY7-internal-USB-2-0-Hub::3419.html

  • not enough USB 2.0 headers on ROG STRIX X870-I GAMING WIFI

Aqua Computer farbwerk 360

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Monitoring-and-Controlling/farbwerk-for-RGBpx/farbwerk-360::3798.html

  • needed non-proprietary 5V ARGB headers for SC ResPro 120 D5 + TG DeltaMate GPU Block Astral + TG Mycro Direct-Die Pro
  • Aquasuite support

Aqua Computer Temperature sensor G1/4

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Monitoring-and-Controlling/Sensors/Temperature-sensor-G1-4::3126.html

  • I'm not entirely sure how autonomous the communication between Aqua Computer devices really is
    • aquasuite doesn't support linux and I want a software independant temperature readout for the fan curves of the Aqua Computer Octo

Aqua Computer Double Protect Ultra

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Water-Cooling/Coolant/Double-Protect-Ultra-5l-canister::3127.html

  • I needed non-opaque liquid for low maintenance
  • if Thermal Grizzly uses it internally I'll be using it too

Aqua Computer EPDM

https://shop.aquacomputer.de/Water-Cooling/Fittings-Tubes/System-16-10-mm/Hose-Aqua-Computer-16-10-mm-EPDM-black::4069.html

  • looked good and I heard it fits well with TG DeltaMate Fittings

EK Torque Drain Valve

https://www.ekwb.com/shop/ek-quantum-torque-drain-valve-satin-titanium

  • clean drain valve
  • screwed into a 14mm TG DeltaMate Fittings and mounted on the bottom Alphacool NexXxoS HPE-30 (18fpi)
  • a hole was drilled into Jonsbo D31 Mesh Screen to mount it
    • I did a terrible job lining up the hole since I drilled the initial hole before owning a proper step drill and thought I'll be filing anyway. That's why it looks so sketch

Fails

CPU:

  • I did a horrible job cleaning up the cpu die after delidding
  • I now have thermal paste all over my smds. probably some lm too
    GPU:
  • I wiped the DeltaMate Backplate with an alcohol wipe (I have subtle white stripes now)
  • Trying to screw down the PCB to the waterblock I noticed that the PCB was slightly misaligned (I had to loosen screws around the already compressed PTM on the core)
  • After the misalignement issue I noticed that I forgot the 12VHPWR thermal pad (I had to loosen all screws again and order a new PTM Pad)
  • I put putty on the chokes instead of the MOSFETs
    • I ordered 100g of putty and remade it
      Loop:
  • I disassembeled the loop because I had a leak
    • The leak was at the drain port (easiest location to access)
      Filling Loop:
  • I ordered a JST 5Pin to USB A cable because I thought the Aqua Computer D5 NEXT wouldn't run on only SATA Power
    • I realized the external psu just wasnt powerful enough
  • D5 didn't begin spinning
    • The pump motor was broken and eventually repaired by aqua computer
      Cables:
  • Broken CableMod Molex Cable
    • The rgb components were fading and the fans weren't spinning since the Aqua Computer Octo reported insufficient voltage levels
    • I had to drain, open the loop, and pull out the gpu to get to the molex connector the cable was plugged into (Aqua Computer farbwerk 360)
      Display Port Cables:
  • I ordered random uhbr20 cables thinking they could handle 4k 240hz
  • had to order vesa certified cables
